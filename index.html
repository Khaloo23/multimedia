About Us

StateCity Football Club is dedicated to inspiring young athletes through discipline, teamwork, and excellence in football. We believe in holistic development on and off the field.

Our Academy

StateCity Football Club Academy offers structured training programs for the following age groups:

Under 8

Our youngest players focus on foundational skills, coordination, and love for the game.

Under 10

Training emphasizes individual skills, basic tactics, and sportsmanship.

Under 13

Players develop technical skills, teamwork, and game strategy understanding.

Under 15

Advanced training focuses on competitive readiness and mental resilience.
